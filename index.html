Rucksack Magazine is a printed bi-annual photography and travel publication dedicated to storytelling. We began in 2016 as an online journal and have recently printed our eighth volume of the magazine called Coast. We have also recently published our first hardback book called Elements, made in partnership with Lannoo Publishers. Our magazines, books and zines feature photo essays, travel stories and fictional pieces exploring a particular theme for each issue. We work as a platform for new and emerging photographers and writers, connecting a global community of creative storytellers.
